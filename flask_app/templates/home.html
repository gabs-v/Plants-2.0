Not sure what to plant right now?
ROMAINE CALM! Here's some awesome things to plant right now!

Carrots

If you live in a cooler climate, carrots are a great veggie to start planting around July! A great thing about them is that they can keep in the soil relatively well until you havest them. Just be sure to not leave them long enough for the little critters and harsh winters get to them before you can enjoy them!

Tomatillos

Oh how tomatillos are a personal favorite! These small, green fruit are most commonly used in Mexican dishes or other hispanic salsas. When you start the seeds, keep them inside for about six to eight weeks before planting it into soil about three feet apart. In about 90 days, get ready to pick them!

Spinach

Due to its low maintenance and quick to mature nature, spinach is a great veggie to plant for both beginners and seasoned gardeners alike! Spinach is one of those awesome veggies that has a small window between planting and harvesting time. It's a veggie that you'll most-definitely want on your plate this season!

Green beans

Suprisingly, green beans have a pretty short growing span. They mature in about 60 days or so. This goes without a warning to not let the soil to dry out as the beans start to bloom. Either way, it's still such a rewarding harvest when the time comes.

Sweet Corn

Sweet corn is a tougher one to grow in the summer compared to others. It just requires more space to grow so about a foot or so between should be good enough. Sweet corn also has shorter roots that can't tolerate dry soil. Make sure to not skip watering it!

Cucumbers

Cucumbers are such a kitchen staple in many households just imagine it staight from your own garden! These do best when there's lots of sun to soak up and well-draining soil. Just wait a good 60-100 days and you'll have some yummy cucumbers to snack on.
